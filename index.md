
Privacy Policy — Croatian Phrases – Kozo
Last updated: June 2026

Croatian Phrases – Kozo ("the App") is developed by KozoTechnology. This
Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information when you use it.

1. DATA WE DO NOT COLLECT
   The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable
   information (PII). We do not ask for your name, email, location, or any
   account information.

2. DATA STORED LOCALLY ON YOUR DEVICE
   The App stores your phrase history, preferences, and notification settings
   locally on your device using Android shared storage. This data never leaves
   your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

3. ADVERTISING (Google AdMob)
   The App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google may collect
   device identifiers and usage data to serve personalised or contextual ads in
   accordance with Google's Privacy Policy:
   https://policies.google.com/privacy

   You can opt out of personalised advertising via your device settings under
   Google > Ads > "Opt out of Ads Personalisation."

4. PERMISSIONS
   - INTERNET: Required for loading advertisements.
   - RECORD_AUDIO / MICROPHONE: Used only for the in-app voice recording
     feature. Recordings are stored locally on your device and are never
     uploaded.
   - POST_NOTIFICATIONS: Used to send the Daily Phrase notification.
   - SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM: Used to schedule daily phrase notifications at
     your chosen time.
   - RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED: Used to restore scheduled notifications after
     device restart.

5. THIRD-PARTY SDKS
   The App uses the following third-party SDKs:
   - Google AdMob (advertising)
   - Flutter TTS (text-to-speech, fully on-device)
   - flutter_local_notifications (local notifications only)
   - home_widget (Android home-screen widget)

6. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY
   The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect
   any data from children.

7. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY
   We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be
   reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

8. CONTACT
   If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:
   kozo.technology@gmail.com
